OUR STORY

A Stay You Will Remember

Tucked away on Kunjaban Path off the Dibrugarh Bypass Road, The Mahalaxmi Inn is a peaceful urban retreat built for travellers who value cleanliness, comfort, and genuine hospitality. Whether visiting for business or leisure, our attentive staff and well appointed rooms ensure you feel right at home.

HOTEL POLICIES

Good to Know Before You Stay

House Rules

  • Check-in: 12:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Valid government-issued ID required at check-in
  • Couples are welcome (local and outstation guests)
  • Pets are welcome on the property
  • Children of any age are welcome
  • Children up to 5 years stay free on existing beds
  • Outside food and alcohol allowed in rooms responsibly
  • Please maintain noise levels after 10:00 PM

Important Notes

  • PAN card is not accepted as ID proof
  • Early check-in and late check-out subject to availability
  • Rooms are non-smoking — smoking permitted in designated areas
  • Kindly inform the front desk of special requests
  • Hotel not responsible for unattended valuables
  • Power backup available during outages
  • Management reserves right to refuse service
